We Started With One Truck and a Big Idea

Junk B Gone was started by Florida native Carlos Rivera in 2018 after he noticed how hard it was for homeowners and businesses to get reliable, affordable junk removal without the runaround.

With a single pickup truck and a passion for helping his community, Carlos built Junk B Gone from the ground up. Today, we operate a fleet of trucks across Florida and have helped over 10,000 customers reclaim their spaces.

We believe junk removal should be simple, transparent, and done right. That means upfront pricing, background-checked crews, and a commitment to donating and recycling as much as possible.

Because when your junk is gone, your life gets a little lighter. That's the Junk B Gone promise.
